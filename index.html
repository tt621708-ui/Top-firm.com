How we protect your funds

We use bank-grade controls, separation of client assets, and strict internal procedures. Below are the technical & compliance practices we recommend you implement before accepting client funds:

  • Always use HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Do not send sensitive data over HTTP.
  • Perform server-side validation and sanitization for every form submission.
  • Store personal data encrypted-at-rest (e.g., AES-256) and never store plaintext payment credentials.
  • Implement KYC/AML onboarding and ID verification for all investors.
  • Use CSRF tokens, strong session management, password hashing (bcrypt/argon2), and role-based authorization.
  • Work with PCI-DSS-compliant payment processors if you accept card payments; do not handle raw card numbers on your server when possible.
  • Maintain an audit trail and offer clear reporting to clients.
